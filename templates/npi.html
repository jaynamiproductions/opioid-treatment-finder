National Provider Identifier Validation Tool (Beta)


4The National Provider Identifier (NPI) is a unique identification number for covered health care providers. 5HIPAA requires providers to adopt and utilize a unique NPI when transmitting health information electronically. 6Medicare requires enrolled providers to have an NPI.

Several measures are put in place to prevent healthcare fraud. In rare occurences, fake NPIs may be used to bypass certain billing or prescribing requirements.

7The National Committee for Information Technology Standards (NCITS) developed a standard to create unique NPIs using the Luhn check digit formula.

This application will validate a given NPI based on the Luhn formula.
